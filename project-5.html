MyWonderBooks

Description

WonderBooks is a Free reading app that makes reading more fun and entertaining. Reading a story book in a native language has its own pleasure that is why in WonderBooks we have all our stories in 6 languages so user can choose the language of the story book they prefer. User can read every story in English - Arabic - French - Russian - German - Spanish all translations for the story books are done by top translators , all done by human to make sure it is well revised and fit understanding. Also translated story book help readers to read same book in different languages to learn new words and sentences.

After a user read a book the user can take a quiz about the story book they just read , the questions in the quiz comes from the story as a comprehension each story have a score of 10 and each user can have a total score for all the books they have read as a record , this encourage the user to read more as there is a reward that the user can keep a track.
