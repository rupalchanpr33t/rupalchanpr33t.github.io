Coravida: A world changing app Use the Coravida app to manage your loyalty points. For all kinds of things within the Coravida ecosystem. There's two ways to earn points: 1. Work on those projects to earn Coras. 2. Donate to projects while helping the world.

Users will be able to send donation using their Paypal account. Coras are points used to redeem for rewards. Their value varies depending on rewards offered.
