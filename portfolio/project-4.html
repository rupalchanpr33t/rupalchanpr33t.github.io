QuantowGear

Description

QuantowGear is the most sophisticated micro race car on the market. Small enough to keep in your pocket and race anywhere!

App controls your car with 3 control modes, Tilt to steer, on screen Joystick, and Race Mode. Have fun racing this car around obstacle courses and race tracks you setup on your desk! Makes a Great Gift!

Technology

  • core motion
  • core bluetooth
