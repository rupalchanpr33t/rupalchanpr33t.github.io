Rythmos

Description

The Rythmos iOS app supports telehealth video chats with a physician as well as multi-party video chats with family members.

Rythmos is an innovative 3-in-1 safety and wellness solution for senior communities. It provides nurse call, wander management, and access control in one wearable.
