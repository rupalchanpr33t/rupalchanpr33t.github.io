Afrobeats On The Pad

Description

AfrobeatsOnThePad is the first Universal app on AppStore for Afrobeat for live performance. It's also a universal app that can be used by DJs, Beatmakers, Live performance beat sequencing. Practice beat and lots more. AfrobeatsOnThePad gives you the power to get your beats on your portable iPad and take any to perform. Just see this app as your portable beat band.

AfrobeatsOnThePad comes with fantastic features like Tap Tempo, Slide Tempo, BPM sync, Volume Normalizer, Itunes import, multiple session creation, and lots more.
