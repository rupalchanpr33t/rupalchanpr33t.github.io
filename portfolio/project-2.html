Description

The Quality mobile application will be used by S10 customers to monitor construction activities at their sites. The application will also track the status of activities that are carried out at multiple sites. The construction site can be a road, an overpass, a building, a shopping center, etc.

The proposed mobile application will act as a platform through which users can keep track of activities at a construction site.
